About Us

More Than a Cut.
It's a Tradition.

Cut N Shave is Niles' most-loved barbershop — a clean, modern space where skilled hands and genuine care come together. Whether it's your first visit or your hundredth, you're always welcome here.

✂

Skilled Barbers

Dan, Sam, and Memo bring years of expertise and passion to every cut. Dan alone brings over a decade of barbering excellence.
